					Hwoarang Anti Guide

1.General Moves: Most of Hwoarang  general moves can be sidestepped to the left (SSL).

2.After Getting Hit by d3,4: If you get hit by d3,4, you can sidestep to the left (SSL) to avoid both the Sky Rocket and another d3,4 follow-up.

  By sidestepping left, you can effectively evade some of Hwoarang common and dangerous follow-up attacks.
